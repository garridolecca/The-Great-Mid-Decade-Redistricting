The Great Mid-Decade Redistricting of 2026

In an unusual move for 2026, several U.S. states are redrawing their congressional maps mid-decade. This interactive map visualizes how redistricting reshapes political representation at every scale, from statewide overviews down to individual streets.

Why it matters A single street-level change in a district boundary can flip a district's political leaning, affecting representation for hundreds of thousands of people.

Key States

  • California (Prop 50) — Legislature-drawn map aims to shift House control
  • Texas — Redraws favor Republicans, sparking legal battles
  • North Carolina — Reversed precedent enables partisan gerrymandering

How to Use

  • Click state buttons to zoom to focus areas
  • Click any district for demographic and political details
  • Zoom to street level to see boundary precision
  • Toggle layers and adjust opacity in the Layers panel
